About Food Finder

Project Description

Food Finder is a web platform to help users discover recipes that align with their dietary needs, allergies, and preferences. It is a solution to the challenge of accessing quick and accurate recipes while managing health concerns and ingredient restrictions.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to provide users with a user-friendly experience in finding recipes tailored to their unique needs. By simplifying the search process and displaying only relevant options, Food Finder encourages users to make healthier food choices with confidence.

Information Problem Being Solved

Many individuals face difficulties finding recipes that meet specific dietary restrictions, preferences, or allergen needs. Existing platforms often lack proper filtering or require significant effort to determine if a recipe is suitable. Food Finder addresses this problem by:

  • Providing accurate recipe filtering based on allergies, dietary restrictions, and preferences.
  • Offering detailed ingredient and nutritional information for informed decision-making.
  • Recommending popular and trending recipes to inspire meal planning.
